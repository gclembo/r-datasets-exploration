R Programming Built in Dataset Exploration

Miles Per Gallon (mpg)

mpg Markdown

The mpg dataset includes data from various cars. In this exploration I fit a linear model to predict city fuel efficiency based on other variables.

Nile River Annual Flow

Nile Markdown

The Nile dataset contains information about the annual water level of the Nile from 1871 to 1970. In this exploration I use a sample from the dataset to estimate the true mean water level over this time interval.

Vapor Pressure of Mercury

pressure Markdown

The Pressure dataset contains pressure and temperature measurements for mercury. In this exploration I fit a non-linear model to the curve to model the relationship between the two variables.
